Our Sectors

Before joining forces to create HAR, the firm’s partners independently built their own market research firms (Coras Research and Infill Thinking). Each of these platforms established long track records of accurate forecasts and influential publishing in energy markets. HAR's approach is a hybrid research model incorporating data service, equity research, and thematic market intelligence.

IMPACTFUL RESEARCH

Utility-Scale Solar

Predictive reports and data leveraging satellite intel. Bottoms-up modeling of grid scale solar project pipeline in reall-time. Utilization tracking for installed base. Tracker market intelligence and forecasting.

Wind Power

Offshore wind project and supply chain data and reports. Onshore wind farm modeling - generation, utilization, degradation, and resource.

Shale Production & Services

Comprehensive modeling and forecasting of key shale drivers: spending, wells, rigs, frac crews, sand, mines, logistics and more. Predictive reports incorporating satellite & boots-on-the-ground intel.

Residential Solar

Weekly permit data for 70% of the U.S. market. State-by-state tracking and forecasting for installations. Predictive model for generation and capacity. Reports on market trends.

Energy Storage Systems

(BTM & FTM) : Bottoms-up project modeling and forecasting in the grid scale battery storage market (standalone and collocated). Cost models and near real-time project tracking. Predictive market reports incorporating satellite intel.
